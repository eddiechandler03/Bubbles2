About Us

Bubbles Animation and Entertainment is a leading Malta-based entity with extensive experience and a proven track record within its field. Our aim is to provide interested parties with quality entertainment programmes executed by experienced and professionally-trained animators both local and foreign. Moreover, an interesting selection of foreign artistes may be provided in collaboration with our overseas contacts, whilst still retaining an excellent rapport with all leading local artistes. Bubbles Animation and Entertainment team operates widely locally whilst it is also penetrating foreign markets such as Italy, Spain, Cyprus and France. Whilst constantly striving to offer the best animators to our esteemed clientele, our team boasts of a wide portfolio of professionally-trained animators with vast experience in the hospitality industry.
